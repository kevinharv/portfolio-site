About Me

Background

Some believe me to be part computer. Although I often cultivate successful relationships with coworkers and companions, alas I must admit I am quite robotic at times. I am opportunistic and driven, constantly pushing the edge of innovation and thought. I seek a higher plane of existence, a place beyond this realm. Intersellar is not a work of science fiction, but rather a prophecy of the future.

Aspirations

As a Computer Science student, the areas of development that I have the opportunity to pursue many career paths. The field of study is far more broad than anyone imagined just ten years ago. That being said, there are a couple specific areas of study that fascinate me. Computer networks for one are a huge area of interest for me. The way in which computers talk to each other, route traffic, tag the information we all place on the internet is astounding. I also have a substantial interest in back-end software design. Manipulating data, connecting systems, creating services, developing the systems that support the applications we all know and love. Even within these two areas, there are many different specialties. Alas, I am hesitant to limit my scope at this point in my career.
